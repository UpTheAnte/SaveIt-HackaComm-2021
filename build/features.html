Leaderboard

We offer a competitive leaderboard that displays electricity reduction of all users in your region. You also have the ability to add friends to your leaderboard to foster healthy competition while reducing your energy consumption!

Track Usage

We provide two options for our users to track their electricity consumption. You can either input total electricity usage every day by using the smartmeter in your home, or we can calcuate the total energy used after you provide us with the time you used each electronic device in your home and their respective wattage.
